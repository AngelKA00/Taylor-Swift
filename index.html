Albums

American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift has released 10 original studio albums, three re-recorded studio albums, five extended plays, and four live albums. She has sold an estimated 114 million album units worldwide and, in terms of pure sales, tallied 37.3 million in the United States and 3.34 million in the United Kingdom.

About

Taylor Alison Swift (born December 13, 1989) is an American singer-songwriter. Swift began professional songwriting at age 14 and signed with Big Machine Records in 2005 to become a country singer.
